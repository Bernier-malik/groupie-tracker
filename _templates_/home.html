Groupie Tracker

Welcome to Groupie Tracker! This is a simple web application that allows you to track your favorite bands and their upcoming concerts. You can search for bands, view their upcoming concerts, and add them to your list of favorite bands.

Click the "Search" link in the navigation bar to get started!
